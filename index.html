The Guardian's Oath

This oath is a call to action for those working in emerging fields with the potential to significantly impact humanity. It emphasizes the importance of responsible development and use of these powerful technologies to ensure they benefit humanity.

The Oath

I do solemnly swear,

  • To uphold the potential of progress: I shall strive to utilize my knowledge and skills in these emerging fields for the betterment of humanity, promoting peace, health, and well-being.
  • To act with foresight and caution: I shall meticulously consider the potential consequences of my work, ensuring it serves the greater good and avoids unintended harm.
  • To prioritize safety and security: I shall prioritize safeguards and responsible development practices to prevent misuse of these technologies by those with malicious intent.

Specific Considerations

  • Quantum Computing

    I shall not exploit the power of these machines for malicious purposes, such as cyberwarfare or privacy breaches. I shall actively research and implement security protocols to safeguard quantum systems from unauthorized access. I shall advocate for open access and collaboration in quantum research, fostering trust and transparency in this field.

  • Genetic Engineering

    I shall respect the sanctity of human life and uphold ethical principles in genetic modification. I shall not create genetic material that could potentially harm humans, either directly or indirectly. I shall not engage in practices that could create human life for discriminatory purposes or with unforeseen consequences. I shall advocate for open discussions and public involvement in shaping the ethical framework of human genetic engineering.

  • To be transparent and accountable for my actions.
  • To collaborate with others who share these values to ensure responsible advancement.
  • To never cease to learn and strive to understand the ethical implications of these powerful technologies.

May progress serve humanity, and may knowledge be used for good.

This oath I take freely, solemnly, and with all sincerity.
